Our Beliefs

At the Church of the Nazarene, we believe in the power of community and the strength of faith. Founded in 1988 by Reverend R. Maxwell, our church has been a beacon of hope for generations. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone can grow spiritually.

Our mission is to spread love and compassion throughout the world. Join us as we work together to make a positive impact on the lives of others.

Our vision is a world filled with kindness, understanding, and acceptance. We value diversity and inclusivity, embracing people from all walks of life. Together, we can build a brighter future for ourselves and for generations to come.

Common Questions

What is your community like?

We are a vibrant community that welcomes people from all walks of life. Our services provide spiritual guidance and foster a sense of belonging.

How can I participate?

You can participate in our weekly services, join small groups, attend special events, and volunteer for various outreach programs. There are many ways to connect and contribute.

Do you have youth programs?

Yes, we offer a range of programs for children and teenagers, including Sunday school, youth groups, and summer camps. Our programs are designed to nurture their faith and provide a supportive environment

Are there small groups?

Absolutely! We have several small groups that focus on different interests and age groups. It’s a great way to connect with others and build meaningful relationships.

What events are coming up?

You can find information about upcoming events on our website calendar, in our weekly bulletin, and on our social media channels. Stay connected to never miss an opportunity.
